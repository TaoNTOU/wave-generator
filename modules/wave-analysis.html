Pierson-Moskowitz (PM) Fully developed sea spectrum, often used when wind has blown long enough over sufficient fetch.
JONSWAP Fetch-limited sea spectrum with a sharper peak controlled by gamma; common for growing wind seas.

TMA Finite-depth spectrum based on JONSWAP with shallow-water transformation, used for coastal water depths.

名詞定義與比對原則 Terminology

  • H1/3：零切法取得所有個別波高後，取最高三分之一波高之平均；常作為時域 significant wave height。
  • Hm0 = 4√m0：由頻譜零階矩 m0 計算之顯著波高；理論上常與 Hs、H1/3 接近。
  • Tz：零上切或零下切平均週期，為時域平均週期。
  • Tp：頻譜尖峰週期，Tp = 1 / fp；不規則波中通常不可直接拿 Tz 與 Tp 比。
  • Tm01、Tm02：由頻譜矩計算的平均週期；Tz 通常較適合與 Tm02 對照。
